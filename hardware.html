Game Developers Don't Need Much

When it comes to hardware, game developers do not need much. You can even create a game on a standard laptop
if you want to.

The Main Parts

The Video Card

The video card is what allows the computer to who what you do on the screen or monitor. For game
development you don't need a high-end video card to do what you want. It is suggested that you look
for a card with at least 4 gigabytes.

Processor

When it comes to processoers people usually prefer some like i5 to i8 processors from Intel.
Intel is known to have good quality hardware that lasts a long time. i5 is known better for minimal
use while i8 is considered a more high-end style of processor. Whichever one you decide to go with
you can make game development work with what you have.

RAM

RAM is what stores a lot of your information on your computer. So when developing a lot of your
your work will be held somewhere when you save the files. The downside is that RAM is limited on
all devices so it would be better to find a computer with a good amount starting off to maybe save
yourself some money down the line. Typically 16-32 gigabytes is the preferred amount.
