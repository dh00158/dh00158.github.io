Here is some general and well known information about the development industry.

There are many facts about gaming that you could go over but I would like to focus on the early stages of games.

The Beginning

Video games first began to rise in popularity in and around the 1970s and at a very rapid rate.
It all started with the game called "Spacewar!" It was a very simplistic game that was developed during 1961 and 1962.
The 1970s is when more programmers started to make more programming languages and started to make more games.
This caused a huge increase in video game sales but also a huge incresae in poorly developed games
because so many people were trying to get games made and dveloped in such quick sucession.

Standard Increase

Since games were being constntly made and with very little time and development it led to a temporary crash
in the gaming market. There were also a mass increase in pirated games being sold all around and forcing the
game developers to increase their standards when it came to development. This ended up leading to the development of
cartridges and eventually to disks and digital purchasing. This change in marketing and selling allowed companies to
better regulate their titles and releases because the game had to be verified and published to make it to store shelves.
