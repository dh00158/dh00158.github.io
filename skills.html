Game Development Has Many Different Positions

Ranging from internal development to marketing and sales game development can require a massive range
of skills.
One of the main jobs of game development is creating the game itself so it requires an extensive range
of creativity to brainstrom ideas and innovate the game. Whether its making a new concept as a whole
or changing already made concepts and giving new ways to make something easier to use or making something better.

Developing Alone Can Be Difficult

Teamwork and the ability to get along with others are both extremely needed skill in the development industry.
This is because of the amount of different positions and specialties that are needed to get a game from the development
to the sales stage and further on.
You also need to have a passion for what you do or else you games are bound to fail. If something is made without a
a drive or passion to make it the best it can be according to the creator's idea it is subject to becoming severly
less than what was intended.

Handling Problems On The Inside

Prgramming is one of the bigger things on the inside of development that needs a pretty big team. There is a lot of
programming involved when it comes to game development so people need bigger teams to help progress faster and help to organize
and problem solve when bugs come up.
Along with that comes the skill of good time management. Time management is a huge thing especially if you are developing
for a big company that has to meet certain deadlines for their investors.
