Developer Insights

Developer insights are articles and forums made by developers for multiple reasons the most popular of those being
articles about upcoming information in the game or upcoming features to be updated or added to the game. The developerforums
provide a place for people of the game's community to come together and ask questions to the developers directly.
There are two main games that stick out to me when it ocmes to how the devlopers interact with their audience and
those are Warframe and Dead By Daylight

Warframe

The developers of Warframe at Digital Extremes often open up forums for people to come ask questions about patches
and updates when they are ready for release. They also listen to player feedback and sometimes implement features or
tweak existing features according to how players respond.

Dead By Daylight

Dave Richard, the creative director for Dead by Daylight did an interview with gaming news website "Gamereactor"
and provided some information on the making and inspiration for the game. He explains that to hopefully keep players
playing they want to keep adding different characters and to update some of the older parts of the game.
